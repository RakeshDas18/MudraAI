About MudraAI

Sattriya Mudra Recognition using AI is a pioneering project that combines the rich cultural heritage of Indian classical dance with the cutting-edge capabilities of artificial intelligence. This model focuses specifically on recognizing single-hand mudras (hand gestures) from the classical Assamese dance form Sattriya, one of the eight major classical dance traditions of India.

Mudras play a vital role in Sattriya, acting as a powerful visual language used to convey emotions, tell stories, and symbolize philosophical concepts. Preserving and digitally interpreting these intricate gestures is not only an exciting challenge but also a step toward safeguarding intangible cultural heritage.
